# Anti-Nuke

Anti-nuke is one of the most important systems in <mark style="color:$danger;">D E A T H</mark>.

## What it helps stop

* Mass bans
* Mass kicks
* Channel deletion spam
* Role deletion spam
* Dangerous permission abuse
* Webhook abuse
* Bot-add abuse

## Example setup

```bash
>antinuke enable
>antinuke punishment quarantine
>antinuke whitelisted add @TrustedStaff
>antinuke logs #security-alerts
```

<details>

<summary><strong>Recommended anti-nuke defaults</strong></summary>

* Keep limits low
* Keep logs enabled
* Whitelist very few people
* Review staff access often

</details>
